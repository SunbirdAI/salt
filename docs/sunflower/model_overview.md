
**Sunflower** is a multilingual large language model designed to support a wide range of Ugandan and regional languages, including:

* Luganda
* Runyankole
* Ateso
* Acholi
* Swahili
* Kinyarwanda
* Runyoro
* Rutooro
* Rukiga
* …and many more

### Key Capabilities

* **Translation:** Translate text between supported languages.
* **Summarization:** Generate concise summaries from longer texts.
* **Explanation:** Provide clear explanations or clarifications.
* **Question & Answer:** Answer general or domain-specific questions.

Sunflower is optimized for multilingual contexts, enabling seamless communication and content generation across Uganda’s diverse languages.
